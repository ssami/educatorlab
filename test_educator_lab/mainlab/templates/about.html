The Educator Lab


The Educator Lab was founded by instructors who realized that children who are not engaged in a classroom are children who are not learning; that rote memorization does not equate to education; and that most teachers have little time to develop resources that will engage, challenge and educate their students.

We know that you are, like us, teachers who care passionately about the development of the children in your classroom. Browse through our resources, send us comments, and feel free to submit your own ideas for future resources.

The need to educate our children beyond quadratic formulae and historical facts is rapidly developing into a movement. We hope that ultimately, the resources we provide on this site will help children to not just learn, but learn how to educate themselves in the future.
